An idea for a restaurant called "Normal Meal." The restaurant would serve one meal every night – cooked for everyone who had dinner at the restaurant. The efforts of the restaurant staff would instead be devoted to cooking - to order- any dessert a guest could think of. Normal meal = the dinner + dessert of your choice.
